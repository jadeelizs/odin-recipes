Spaghetti

Description

a white, starchy pasta of Italian origin that is made in the form of long strings, boiled, and served with any of a variety of meat, tomato, or other sauces.

Ingredients

  • Linguine
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Parmesan
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Boil pot of water
  2. Add linguine to boiling water for 8 minutes or until al dente
  3. Heat sauce in pan for roughly 5 minutes
  4. Plate cooked spaghetti, add sauce on top and garnish with parmesan and basil
