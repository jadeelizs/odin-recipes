Ramen

Description

A Japanese noodle soup, with a combination of a rich flavoured broth, one of a variety of types of noodle and a selection of meats or vegetables, often topped with a boiled egg.

Ingredients

  • Udon Noodles
  • Chicken
  • Egg
  • Bok Choy
  • Chicken Broth
  • Scallions
  • Sesame Seeds

Steps

  1. Season chicken and bake in oven at 400 degrees for 20 minutes
  2. Bring broth to a simmer in meantime, add choise of seasoning
  3. In a seperate pot, boil water and add egg for 6 minutes, then give the egg a ice bath
  4. Heat noodles in said simmering broth until soft
  5. Add in Bok Choy to simmering broth with noodles
  6. After chicken is cooked, cut up in bite size strips, then assemble in broth
  7. Add peeled and cut egg to ramen and garnish with scallions!
